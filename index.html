J.A. Dancik Electric

Doing Business the Right Way

The Quality You Deserve

Ohio native Jeff Dancik became an Electrician in 1981 and quickly worked his way up to Master Electrician by 1985. After moving to Arizona in 1987 and working with many different electrical contractors (union and non-union), as well as the Electrical Department for the City of Phoenix, he decided to start his own company. In the year 2000, J.A. Dancik Electric was born.

Today, the staff at J.A. Dancik Electric understands the importance of adhering to the highest quality standards in every project we take on. Since our founding over 20 years ago, our expertise and careful craftsmanship has allowed us to provide long-lasting solutions for our valued clients.

Our Services

Equipped with a C11 License and 20+ years in business, the staff at J.A. Dancik Electric is prepared to help you in all phases of electrical work.

Residential

With so many electrical contractors out there, you may find it difficult to be sure you’re selecting the right one as you search. Whether you need assitance with electrical wiring, relays, switches or control devices for lighting, power outlets, or appliances, your search ends here.

Commercial

With decades of experience and numerous satisfied customers, you can count on us to install, design, and maintain electrical systems in commercial buildings. We specialize in wiring instruments that control the power, lighting and heating units in buildings, as well as providing wiring for air conditioning and refrigeration units.
